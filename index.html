About Gram

Gram is a programming language designed to help you take your first steps into the world of programming.

We believe that coding should be a basic skill that every student has access to. Gram was developed to help students in primary school and early levels of secondary school to learn the basics of code. We are aimed to elimate the confusion of syntax/grammatical errors that students face when they code. Because of Gram's similarity to the English language, students will have a seemless experience with this language, allowing students to get familiar with the basics of coding.

Designers.

We are a group of high schoolers.

We are a group of developers that identified a problem in programming, that problem is that students don't have a good beginner level program to enter the world of coding. Today platforms such as "Scratch" exist, however, we cannot recognize them as true coding because they don't incorperate the text-based nature and other important properties of actual programing languages.
